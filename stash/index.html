Stash

Stash is an app for quickly dumping things for later into folders from the share menu. I designed it so that I could quickly store photos of restaurants I wanted to go to, books I wanted to read, and events I want it to go to. This grew a little bit to include links and text, before expanding into implementing a reader mode for articles and a text to speech reader.

It's local only, and somewhere between Pocket and Pinterest.

See it on GitHub.
